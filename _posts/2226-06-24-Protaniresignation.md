Voltaya district leader Elmer Protani announced his resignation late last night.
He has been facing backlash and pressure to step down from his post after a string of controversal policy decisions and public statements regarding Kolay natives returning to the City and their attempts to regain some power in the Voltaya (Kolaya) district.

> "Avalon is an inclusive and tolerant City, but the public expect that people who come here should be expected to learn the language and integrate. Without rules, we risk becoming an city of strangers, not one that walks forward together."
> -Elmer Protani (A, VCW) last week at a Volt Corp Workers representatives meeting

His comments have been criticized broadly by both VCW members and representatives from other parties.
