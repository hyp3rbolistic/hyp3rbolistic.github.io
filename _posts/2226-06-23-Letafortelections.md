The numbers are in, and progressive candidates running in Letafort's next election are coming out on top. Roliee Manson (L, WRK) took a commanding lead early on in the District Leader race, edging out over long term party big-shot Riand Baltyn. At the local level, early polling shows large gains from VCW, WRK, SOC, and KYR, with every WRK candidate leading in their sector.

This election is a major test of the district's progressive momentum that's been building leading up to the City's 100th anniversary. VCO and AHP leaders have invested 4.9 billion into the celebration so far, with at least 2 billion more expected to be spent before the big day. 

Manson is a political outsider, with her first foray into the Assembly was just last year when she sat in as a WRK secretary. Her radical ideas and policy proposals make her popular with young Avalonians as well as Kolays who appreciate her staunch committment to supporting their anti-colonialist cause.

Volt Corp leaders have criticised Manson's campaign, saying she continues to stoke dissidents and encourage protestors to clash with public safety officers. Her team did not immediately respond to a request for comments.
